Candles remain popular even today despite the fact that they were primarily used for illumination purposes. They have been there for centuries and used in different civilizations and cultures. With the introduction of electricity the role of candles has been redefined. Uniquely branded candles are the most popular choice of home decor and as such are very high on demand. Candles are known for their soothing and healing properties, often used by therapists, such as massage therapists, to create a peaceful ambiance in their therapy rooms. The sheer act of lighting a candle, its lovely flicker and the gentle glow it creates, helps these and soothe the mind. The era of scented candles and fragrances has ushered in a perspective and the market constantly looking for unique and creatively made candles for use in homes, offices and special occasions.
